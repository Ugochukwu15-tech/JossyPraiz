Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from JossyPraiz

Home/FAQ

You can place an order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and checking out. Alternatively, you can order via WhatsApp by clicking the WhatsApp button on any product or using the floating chat icon.

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash on delivery (within Lagos). For online orders, you'll receive payment details via WhatsApp after placing your order. We'll confirm your payment before shipping.

Delivery within Lagos takes 1-2 business days. For orders outside Lagos, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. We ship to all 36 states in Nigeria.

Delivery costs ₦2,000 flat rate nationwide. For bulk orders above ₦50,000, we offer free delivery within Lagos and discounted rates for other states.
