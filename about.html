Our Story

JossyPraiz Textile was born out of a passion for quality fabrics and the desire to make premium materials accessible to every Nigerian — whether you're a fashion designer, tailor, or style enthusiast.

We started with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to beautiful, durable, and affordable fabrics. Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across all 36 states, offering everything from vibrant Ankara prints to luxurious senator cashmere and elegant lace materials.

Our team personally handpicks every fabric we sell, ensuring it meets our strict quality standards. We believe in the power of Nigerian fashion and want to support the designers and artisans who bring it to life.
